The M135i was first introduced in the year 2012 and has been standing out from all of its rivals. The M135i offers a 6 cylinder twinpower turbo that gives the driver an amazing experience with sound and the linear power delivery. It offers rear wheel drive for some fun in a trackday, but dont let that put you off as its adaptive suspensions have been proven to be the best out of its rivals!. 320bhp is more than enough for a rear wheel drive but there are so many tunning opportunities that will allow this car to get to around 500bhp with very few add on parts such as exhaust, intercooler, air-filter, etc. Consequently, BMW’s effort has had the breathing space to establish itself as the benchmark premium mega-hatch. With a starting price of just over £30,000 and 315bhp from its turbocharged six-cylinder engine, the rear wheel drive M135i promises a massive bang for your buck.
